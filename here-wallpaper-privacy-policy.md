# Privacy Policy

**Here Wallpaper** · Locusable Studio · Effective July 12, 2026

Here Wallpaper (“the App”) is made by Locusable Studio (“we”, “us”). **We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.**

## What we don’t collect

We do not operate accounts, analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or our own servers that receive your information. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing.

## Data that stays on your device

The App may use system features on your iPhone or iPad. This data is processed on your device and is not sent to us:

- **Location** — optional, to place the map near you or a chosen place when creating a wallpaper.
- **Photo Library** — optional, only when you save an exported wallpaper.
- **App settings** — preferences such as theme and layout are stored locally on your device.

## Purchases

Optional Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details. Apple’s privacy practices apply to those transactions.

## Map content

Map tiles and place labels may be loaded from map data providers (for example OpenFreeMap / OpenStreetMap-related services) so the App can display maps. Those requests are made by the App on your device to show map imagery; we do not run a separate backend that collects your identity from them.

## Children

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.

## Contact

Questions about this policy: [locusablestudio@gmail.com](mailto:locusablestudio@gmail.com)

# 隐私政策

**Here Wallpaper** · Locusable Studio · 生效日期：2026 年 7 月 12 日

Here Wallpaper（「本应用」）由 Locusable Studio（「我们」）开发。**我们不收集、不存储、不传输、不出售任何个人数据。**

## 我们不收集什么

我们不提供账号体系，不接入分析、广告或崩溃上报，也不运营会接收你信息的自有服务器。我们不会将个人数据出售或分享给第三方用于营销。

## 仅留在本机的数据

本应用可能使用 iPhone / iPad 的系统能力。相关数据在你的设备上处理，不会发送给我们：

- **位置** — 可选；用于在创建壁纸时将地图定位到你附近或所选地点。
- **照片图库** — 可选；仅在你保存导出的壁纸时使用。
- **应用设置** — 主题、排版等偏好保存在本机。

## 购买

可选的 Pro 订阅由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理。我们不会收到你的支付卡信息。相关交易适用 Apple 的隐私政策。

## 地图内容

地图瓦片与地点标注可能由地图数据服务（例如与 OpenFreeMap / OpenStreetMap 相关的服务）提供，以便显示地图。这些请求由本应用在设备端发起以加载地图画面；我们不运营用于收集你身份的独立后端。

## 儿童

本应用不会有意收集任何人的个人信息，包括 13 岁以下儿童。

## 变更

若本政策有变更，我们将更新本页及上方生效日期。
